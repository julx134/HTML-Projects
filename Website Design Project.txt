<br>
<ul>

<li>I use <a href="http://www.thesaurus.com/">Thesaurus</a> to search up words that I don't understand. It is <em>especially</em> helpful for ESL students because a whole bunch of features is also found in the site like synonyms and acronyms.</li>

<br>
<li>Lastly, I use <a href="http://www.calvin.edu/library/knightcite/">knightcite</a> for citing projects because sometimes it is really hard to keep track and organize links when doing something like an essay or a project, but knightcite keeps things organized and orderly. The teachers recommend it too and its an automatic citation service! </li>
