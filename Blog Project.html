Niagara Falls

Posted on 08/15/2014

We went to Niagara Falls! It was the first time I went to Niagara Falls on August 15, 2014! My first reaction was sort of like a child accidentally getting left behind a candy store. When me and my family got close, I thought it was raining! I got real sad, but then I was wondering why people have these yellow raincoats. It hit me; the falls was so strong that it created its own dense humid atmosphere. I guarantee that anyone close to falls cannot avoid being wet without raincoats. For people who have already seen Niagara Falls, you know what I mean, but for those who have not seen the falls, I can't tell you much but here is a picture of it.



Canada's Wonderland

Posted on 08/30/2014

Nearing the end of summer, my family and I decided to go to Canada's Wonderland! TV ads on CW (Canada's Wonderland) makes it seem small and look like your casual county fare. Boy was I wrong! CW is gigantic! About 70 American Footballl Fields can fit in it! In addition to size, Canada's Wonderland has endless, wave after wave of rides and fun things to do! A simple to-do list won't be enough to fully discover Canada's Wonderland. Like when we went to the waterslide and the scary Leviathan and some games especially Laser Tag. At the end of the day, me and my family ended up in a petting zoo where I got to see many cute and cuddly animals. Every animal was great, but I have to give it to the rabbit.
